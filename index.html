How does "dis-ease" start?

German New Medicine recognises that the body makes no mistakes, only adaptations. Symptoms that have been labeled diseases by modern medicine are smart and meaningful adaptations made by our body designed to help us deal with changes int he environment.

"The Cause"

Unexpected Conflict Shock

A highly acute, unexpected biological conflict shock hits the psyche. These conflicts are completely subject and act on the subconscious mind. From an evolutionary perspective, these conflicts threaten our survival.

"The Adaptation"

Special Biological Programs

In response to this conflict shock the brain orchestrates changes at the organ level. These physiological changes known as Special Biological Programs (SBS) are a smart adaptations made by the body to combat the stressful changes in the environemnt.

"The Healing Phase"

The body returns to homoestatsis

Once the biological conflict has resolved, the adaptations made by the body are no longer required. In its return to homoestatis the body removes the biological adaptations. This process is where most healing criss symptoms are occuring.
