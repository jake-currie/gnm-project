How can GNM Help?

German New Medicine, developed by Dr Hamer shows the link between an unexpected emotional conflicts and physical symptoms.

By understanding the emotional cause of symptoms as an GNM practitioner I am able to help guide you making the connection between your physical symptoms and the emotional cause.

Creating Awareness

The first stage in allowing the body to heal is to create an awareness of the connection between the biological conflict and your symptoms. Often this stage along is enough to see progress.

Breaking Associations

When the inital conflict occurs our subconscious mind goes to work taking note of everything we see, hear, smell, taste and touch. It is perparing to protect us. From a biological perspective, if we expereinced a territorial conflict when we went to a watering hole, our brain will perpare us for battle when we go there next.

Change Perspective

Without awarenesss of the link between your physical symptoms and the emotional conflicts it is difficult to think differently. German New Medicine invites you to change the way you think not only about the initial conflict but also the healing symptoms.
