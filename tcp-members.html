WHAT IS GNM?


"German New Medicine (GNM) is the science of how specific unexpected biological conflicts create physical adaptations in the body to help the organism cope with the stressful environment."


Sounds complex, right? But it is actually very simple.

To explain, think of an antelope...

Let's call him, Anthony.

Now Anthony the antelope is going for a drink at his usual watering hole, when...BAM!!!

NO WATER.

This is an unexpected biological conflict that potentially threatens his existance.

As a result of this stressful environment, his little antelope kidneys start to adapt.


Modern medicine would call these adaptations a DISEASE.


A GNM practitioner KNOWS this "disease" is actually a smart adaptation by the body to help Anthony conserve water.

The changes are helping him survive and cope.
