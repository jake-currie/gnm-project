Where it all began

I was drawn to chiropractic for its philosophy; the body heals from the inside out. Chiropractic at its core recognises that the body has an amazing ability to heal, it works with the body to help these inborn healing abilities. I started university feeling like I was about to change the world, and help people heal naturally! However, my time at university started to change my perspective of this. Chiropractic was feeling the pressure to become more 'mainstream' in an attempt to seek approval from the scienticfic medical community. In doing so, chiropractic began to shy away from these natural laws that it was founded on, what I was originally drawn to, in doing so making us graduates more like physiotherapists.

I began to feel upset with where I felt the profession was going.

My GNM story started at a health and wellness expo in Caloundra. I was there giving a presentation on running technique and talking to people about chiropractic. During the expo I watched a presentation by a man named Dr. Alvin De Leon, a fellow chiropractor from Canada. He spoke about GNM and the Five Biological Laws. His presentation ressonated with something deep inside of me, a deep knowing, a truth. Later after the presentation, I went up and spoke to Dr. Alvin and William Hughes.

It was this conversation with Will that changed my life.

I had a groin strain from CrossFit that was completely debilitating, dispite my best rehabilitation efforts I had not been able to train for around 5 months. Nothing I did seemed to help, I did everything right but this injury just wouldn't budge. I asked Will if GNM can help with pain and muscle tears. He responded with a calm but definitive, yes. In the next 5 minutes of conversation Will took my pain from a 7/10 (squeezing my legs togther) down to a 1/10, just by asking me some specific questions. Sure enough, I was hanging on to a particualr emotional conflict. My awareness around this conflict shifted the pain immediatley. I left the conversation completely dumbfounded!

How can something like that change instantly just from being aware of something I was stressed about?!

That conversation was in May 2014, ever since then I have been studying and learning everything I can about GNM from Will, my mentor, Alvin, Caroline and the Learning GNM resources. As a chiropractor every patient I saw after this initial interation I looked at from a GNM perspective.

This knowledge was a blessing but also a curse. It became increasingly difficult to maintain the status quo of being a chiropractor. Sure, I was having amazing results with patients, but I wanted to work help people entirely with GNM, something that is difficult in a busy chiropractic office. I decided to sell the practice and take some time away.

A year and a half later, I found my happy medium working with people again and helping them with GNM. I am so gratful for the opportunity to help people in this way!
